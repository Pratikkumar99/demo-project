About Ananya

Ananya is a serial entrepreneur with deep expertise in e-commerce and consumer internet businesses. She founded ShopEasy in 2015, which grew to become one of India's leading niche e-commerce platforms before being acquired by Flipkart in 2020.

Prior to ShopEasy, Ananya spent 5 years at Google India leading product teams. She has mentored over 50 startups in the past 3 years, with several going on to raise significant funding.
