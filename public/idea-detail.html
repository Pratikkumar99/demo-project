Idea Description

Our AI-powered recruitment platform revolutionizes hiring by focusing on skills and cultural fit rather than traditional resume screening. The system uses machine learning to analyze candidate capabilities through practical assessments and behavioral analysis, reducing hiring bias and improving retention rates.

Problem We Solve

Traditional recruitment processes are inefficient, biased, and often result in poor hiring matches. Companies spend thousands on hiring only to face high turnover, while qualified candidates get overlooked due to resume screening limitations.

Our Solution

  • Skills-based assessment engine that evaluates actual capabilities
  • Cultural fit algorithm that analyzes work style preferences
  • Bias-reduction technology for more diverse hiring
  • Automated interview scheduling and candidate scoring

Current Status

Prototype - 65% complete

We have a working prototype with core matching algorithms developed. Currently seeking technical co-founder to help scale the platform and UX designer to improve interface.
